# MineIt Diamonds 💎

A browser-based diamond mining casual game inspired by a 2010 game design proposal. Mine through rock, collect diamonds graded on the 4 Cs, upgrade your equipment, and advance through 7 increasingly rich mining sites.

**Play now → [mineitdiamonds.com](https://mineitdiamonds.com)**

---

## Gameplay

Click tiles to mine them — each reveals either ore or a diamond. Harder rock requires multiple hits. Sell diamonds for coins, spend coins on better equipment and tools, and collect enough diamonds to advance to the next level.

### 7 Mining Sites
| Level | Site | Max Carats |
|-------|------|-----------|
| 1 | Alluvial Fields | 0.5ct |
| 2 | Open Pit Mine | 1.2ct |
| 3 | Underground Tunnels | 2.0ct |
| 4 | Deep Cave System | 3.0ct |
| 5 | Volcanic Kimberlite Pipe | 4.5ct |
| 6 | Mantle Depths | 6.0ct |
| 7 | Asteroid Belt | 9.0ct |

### Currencies
- **🪙 Coins** — earned by selling diamonds, spent on equipment and consumables
- **🎟 Scrip** — labor currency, used to hire miners who auto-mine 4 tiles (exchange coins → scrip in the Shop)

### Equipment
| Tool | Diamond Yield | Unlocks |
|------|-------------|---------|
| ⛏️ Hand Pickaxe | 4% | Level 1 |
| 🔩 Steel Drill | 12% | Level 2 |
| 💎 Diamond Drill | 24% | Level 3 |
| ⚡ Laser Cutter | 40% | Level 5 |
| 🔥 Plasma Drill | 62% | Level 7 |

### Consumables
- **💥 Explosive Charge** — blasts a 3×3 area, each tile has 50% diamond chance
- **📡 Deep Scanner** — peek inside a tile before mining it
- **🍀 Lucky Streak** — next 10 mines get +15% bonus diamond chance
- **🔭 Geologist Survey** — reveals all diamond locations on the current grid

### Permanent Upgrades
- **🏭 Ore Refinery** — every empty ore tile earns 2 coins automatically
- **💍 Jewelry Workshop** — combine 3 diamonds into jewelry worth 2.5× their value
- **🧠 Master Geologist** — permanently adds +6% diamond yield on all equipment

### Diamond Grading (The 4 Cs)
Every diamond is graded on Carats, Clarity (FL → I1), Color (D → M, plus rare Fancy colors), and Cut (Excellent → Fair). Higher grades yield higher sell prices. Rare Fancy diamonds — Pink, Blue, Yellow, Green, Red — are worth far more than white diamonds.

## Features
- 🎮 Single HTML file — no install, no dependencies, runs in any browser
- 📱 Mobile responsive — playable on phone and desktop
- 🪨 Tile hardness system — soft, medium, and hard rock with visual crack overlays
- ✨ Cartoonish mining animations per equipment type
- 💥 Explosive blast, radar scanner, lucky streak, geologist survey
- 💍 Jewelry crafting workshop
- 📈 7-level progression with shop items unlocking as you advance
